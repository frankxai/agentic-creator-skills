# Brand Architect Connectors

| Category | Default | Alternatives |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| `~~knowledge base` | Local creator-memory/voice.md | Notion brand guidelines, Confluence |
| `~~design` | Figma | Local visual.md |
| `~~chat` | Slack | Discord, Teams |
| `~~publishing` | Local CREATOR.md | Any document store |

## Connector Usage in This Plugin

- `~~knowledge base` — retrieve full brand voice documentation, style guides, tone examples
- `~~design` — import color palette, typography, visual guidelines to cross-reference with voice
- `~~chat` — send voice review reports or brand audit results to team channel
- `~~publishing` — sync CREATOR.md updates to shared brand knowledge base

## No Connectors Required

Brand Architect stores everything locally:
- `CREATOR.md` — hot cache with voice summary
- `creator-memory/voice.md` — full voice documentation
- `creator-memory/channels.md` — per-channel rules

Works fully offline. Connectors add team collaboration capability.
